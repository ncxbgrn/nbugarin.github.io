About Me

I’m a student at SAIT studying Interactive Design, specializing in web design and development. Based in Calgary, I focus on creating modern, user-centered digital experiences that balance clean aesthetics with functionality.

Through my academic projects, I’ve developed skills in UX design, front-end development, and visual storytelling. I enjoy transforming ideas into engaging interfaces that are both intuitive and visually impactful.

I’m currently expanding my knowledge in responsive design, JavaScript interactions, and user experience strategies, with the goal of building meaningful and polished digital products.
